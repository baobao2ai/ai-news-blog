**AI News Daily** is a curated daily digest of the most important developments in artificial intelligence.

We cover:
- 🔬 Research breakthroughs from labs like OpenAI, DeepMind, Anthropic, Meta AI, and academia
- 🛠️ New tools, frameworks, and open-source releases
- 🏢 Industry news, funding rounds, and company moves
- ⚖️ AI policy, ethics, and regulation

Posts are published daily. Sources include arXiv, TechCrunch, Wired, Hacker News, and official announcements.
